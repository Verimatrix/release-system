What's new

Minimum Joomla 4.2 and PHP 8.0. Remember that Joomla 5 is less than a year away with a minimum PHP version requirement of 8.1.

Dashboard. The old control panel page has been replaced with a native Joomla dashboard. You can add modules to it, or re-arrange items to fit your workflow.

Removed PHP version warnings. Joomla displays its own.

Warnings are now DETAILS elements. More compact, by default they only display a header. Click on the header to see the longer message.

Bug fixes and minor improvements. Please take a look at the CHANGELOG below.
